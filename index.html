How to use the app

  1. Choose the class notes to aim to study.
  2. Choose the corresponding quiz and the difficulty level you want.
  3. Answer the questions and receive feedback and short explanations after each answer.
  4. See your progress during the quiz and your results after completion.

About this app

The app consists of 2 main sections: the full class notes and the quizzes consisting of multiple-choice questions divided into 3 difficulty levels (surface-level, complex/specific, exam-level). The order in which you use the 2 sections is up to your discretion.

The 6 total class notes follow the EU law section of the course LAW101 and are based and, to a certain extent, quote the Professor of the course (Prof. Kristin Henrard). The quizzes were generated by AI based on the class notes and the lecture slides.

Level 1 — surface-level Level 2 — complex / specific Level 3 — exam-level
