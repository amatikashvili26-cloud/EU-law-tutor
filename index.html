How to use the app

  1. Choose a topic and review the full class notes.
  2. Set your study status for each topic: Not started, Reading, Revised, or Mastered.
  3. Use Flashcards to revise key concepts, treaty provisions, and case-law points.
  4. Select a quiz and difficulty level: Level 1 (surface-level), Level 2 (complex/specific), or Level 3 (exam-level).
  5. Answer questions and receive immediate feedback and short explanations.
  6. Use Exam Mode for timed practice with no feedback until the end.
  7. Use Review Mistakes to revisit incorrect questions until they are answered correctly enough to be removed.
  8. Track your progress, results, and topic mastery throughout your study sessions.

About this app

This app is designed as a structured EU law revision tool based on Professor Kristin Henrard's LAW101 course. The 6 total class notes follow the EU law section of the course and are based and, to a certain extent, quote the Professor of the course (Prof. Kristin Henrard).

The app includes:

  • full class notes organised by topic
  • flashcards (generated by AI) for active recall and quick revision
  • three quiz difficulty levels (generated by AI): surface-level, complex/specific, and exam-level
  • study status tracking for each topic: Not started, Reading, Revised, Mastered
  • Exam (generated by AI) Mode for timed, no-feedback assessment
  • Review Mistakes mode for repeating incorrect answers
  • progress and performance tracking across study sessions
